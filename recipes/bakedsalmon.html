Baked Salmon

Description

This simple yet refined salmon dish is an easy way to impress your dinner guests!

No frills here, this dish includes a lightly seasoned salmon and fried up potatoes and a side of kale. Cook time 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb salmon (skin on)
  • 4 golden potatoes
  • big bowl of fresh kale
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • butter
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Season salmon with salt and pepper
  3. place on baking dish or sheet and put in oven for 20-25 minutes
  4. peel and cube potatoes
  5. heat pan with olive oil and clove of garlic and toss in potatoes
  6. add salt and pepper to taste
  7. when they begin to brown, put 1 tbsp of butter in pan and finish frying
  8. in another pan heat up 1 tbsp of butter, salt and pepper and toss kale in
  9. cook until they begin to wilt but retain their green vibrancy
  10. remove fish from oven, plate dish.
  11. Enjoy!
